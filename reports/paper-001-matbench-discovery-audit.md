Layer B (GPU): regenerate predictions and re-score.

**Status:** Layer A executed for **2 models** — CHGNet and SevenNet-0 — on
`unique_prototypes` + `full_test_set`.

**Finding:** MATCH (2/2). Independent recompute and upstream `stable_metrics` both
reproduce the official YAML metrics exactly for both models (integer confusion counts

(0xC0000005) on the download+compute run, non-reproducible once the file is cached.
