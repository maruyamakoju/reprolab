Layer B (GPU): regenerate predictions and re-score.

**Status:** Layer A executed for **3 models** — CHGNet, SevenNet-0, and MACE-MP-0 — on
`unique_prototypes` + `full_test_set`.

**Finding:** MATCH (3/3). Independent recompute and upstream `stable_metrics` reproduce
the official YAML metrics exactly across all checked models and both audited subsets
(integer confusion counts identical; F1/MAE/RMSE/R² equal to 3 dp; `missing_preds`
counts match — including MACE's 38/34 genuine-NaN case, distinct from the outlier-filter
case of CHGNet 2 / SevenNet 3). Side findings retained: (1) `pred_file_url`
(`figshare.com/files/…`) is WAF-blocked for naive downloaders — the Figshare API
endpoint is required; (2) the PyPI wheel `1.3.1` ships a different module layout than
repo HEAD `1.3.1`; (3) one transient native crash (0xC0000005) on the download+compute
run, mitigated by the `--download-only` split.
